Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Processing Time

Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST on business days (Monday–Friday) are processed and shipped the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST or on weekends and holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping options for domestic (US) orders:

  • Standard Shipping: 3–5 business days — $7.95 (free on orders over $150)
  • Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days — $14.95
  • Priority Overnight: 1 business day — $29.95

Delivery times are estimates from the date of shipment and are not guaranteed. Actual delivery may vary based on carrier conditions and your location.

Free Shipping

Free standard shipping is available on all domestic orders totaling $150 or more before taxes. Free shipping applies automatically at checkout — no coupon code required.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. If you don't receive a tracking email within 24 hours of placing your order, check your spam folder or contact us at support@novypeptideco.com.

Discreet Packaging

All orders are shipped in plain, unmarked packaging with no external branding, logos, or product descriptions. Your privacy is important to us.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship within the United States only. We do not ship to P.O. Boxes for expedited or overnight orders. We reserve the right to decline shipments to certain addresses at our discretion.

Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost, damaged, or shows signs of tampering upon arrival, contact us within 7 days of the expected delivery date at support@novypeptideco.com. Please include your order number, a description of the issue, and photos if applicable. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and may issue a replacement or refund at our discretion.

Address Accuracy

Please double-check your shipping address before completing your order. Novy Peptide Co. is not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If you need to change your shipping address, contact us immediately after placing your order — we can only make changes before the order ships.

Contact

Shipping questions? Email us at support@novypeptideco.com.
