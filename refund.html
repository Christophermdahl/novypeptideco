Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Our Commitment

Novy Peptide Co. stands behind the quality of every product we ship. If a product doesn't meet our documented specifications, we'll make it right — with a replacement or a refund.

Eligibility for Refund or Replacement

You may request a refund or replacement if your product arrives damaged or broken during shipping, a product does not match its documented Certificate of Analysis specifications, or you received the wrong product.

How to Request a Refund

Contact us within 30 days of delivery at support@novypeptideco.com with your order number, a description of the issue, and photos if applicable. Our team will review your request and respond within 2 business days.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Opened, reconstituted, or partially used vials
  • Products not in their original sealed packaging
  • Orders where more than 30 days have passed since delivery
  • Issues caused by improper storage or handling after delivery

Refund Process

Once your return request is approved, refunds will be issued to your original payment method within 5–10 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when the refund is processed. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Replacements

If you prefer a replacement instead of a refund, let us know in your initial request. Replacement orders are shipped at no additional cost and follow our standard shipping timelines.

Damaged in Transit

If your package arrives visibly damaged, take photos of the outer packaging and the contents before discarding anything. Email us at support@novypeptideco.com with the photos and your order number. We'll handle the carrier claim and ship a replacement immediately.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel an order, contact us as soon as possible. Orders that have not yet been processed can be cancelled for a full refund. Once an order has shipped, it cannot be cancelled — you may request a return after delivery.

Contact

Questions about our refund policy? Email support@novypeptideco.com — we're happy to help.
